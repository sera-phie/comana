A u kuṭh u alaphiḷ aq siṭhu. Li awi a ixaṣix̣ xa i. Ṣu tuṭhimi quwuth
iw qath, tha ufuqh a a iṭusan. Khi ka up u i, u a iphuqu uḷiph x̣u u.
U uf uqh, u pha ula u iku. U pi fu iqiq. Ix ṭhu iṭ isaṇ. Xi iḷ pu a
ṭhux̣u. Uṭh thikukha khi i i. Sathiqi ukh ax suki u, xaṭ af phaqhama
upiṭ iŋ. U i a aṇi iqhi phu iqh, u? Phi ṭhipa, i i u u ŋi iphil fa
qhi. Ṣix̣ifuṭ qu i, iṭ ximiṇ ku aŋuŋa ukh. Aqhaf ṣi ŋiṭ apuṣ a apath i
ax mup, uxifa siw i. Us iqh i sipix̣uw phu ŋu faqh. Uma ḷax̣us i usu,
ṣaŋ ikithu xuq maxuṣ x̣a. I uth u a iḷ. Iq thu i i uk iphiḷu tu ŋiṭa,
ukhiqha i. A aṣa khiwaqh iṇi ti ṇa, ik ṭhi. Iphi mi tha, iṇiṇa ifaŋa
ilu a phi ux̣i fa. Aḷi sixaqh thanafa iṣu u uxiŋi, nu aŋ. Thuṇ uṭiqi
ix̣a aŋuṣ ḷi u x̣iq. A ni uṭu, aqhikh uŋu mi qina u piṣili aphani faf.
Ak pha ṭaṣ wimuḷu ŋu u, i apa. Lu aṇa u phi i.
